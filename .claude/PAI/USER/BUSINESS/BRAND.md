# StoicAI Brand Guidelines

## Brand Identity
- **Name:** StoicAI
- **Primary Domain:** stoicai.ca
- **Blog Domain:** agileguy.ca (personal brand / thought leadership)

## Visual Identity
- **Color Palette:**
  - Primary: Deep dark backgrounds (#0a0a0a, #1a1a2e)
  - Accent: Stoic Gold (#c9a84c)
  - Action: Stoic Orange (#ff6b35)
  - Text: Muted warm grays, white for emphasis
  - Cards: Dark card backgrounds with subtle gold borders on hover
- **Typography:**
  - Headings: Serif font (classical, authoritative)
  - Body: Clean sans-serif
  - Quotes: Italic serif
- **Design Language:**
  - Roman numeral numbering for lists (I, II, III, IV, V, VI)
  - Stoic philosopher quotes between sections
  - Gradient dividers with gold accent
  - Minimal, high-contrast, dark theme
  - Cards with border-hover effects

## Voice & Tone
- **Direct.** No fluff, no jargon, no corporate speak.
- **Confident.** 25 years of engineering backs every claim.
- **Service-oriented.** Everything is framed around the charity's mission, not our capabilities.
- **Stoic.** Calm authority. Action over words. Results over promises.
- **Canadian.** CRA, not IRS. Charities, not nonprofits (though we use both for SEO).

## Key Phrases
- "Your operating budget: $0"
- "We're engineers, not consultants who bill by the month"
- "If it doesn't deliver, you don't pay"
- "Virtue is found in action, not intention"
- "The impediment to action advances action"
- "Your people joined to change lives. Not to drown in spreadsheets."

## Stoic Philosophers Referenced
- **Marcus Aurelius** — duty, systems thinking, leadership under pressure
- **Seneca** — generosity, time management, action
- **Epictetus** — service, resilience, doing what must be done
- **Cato** — leading by example, walking alongside those you serve

## Content Guidelines
- Always lead with the charity's pain, not our solution
- Quantify impact (hours saved, cost = $0, 15+ hours/week)
- Include guarantee language
- Reference Stoic philosophy naturally, not forced
- FAQ-style content for common objections
