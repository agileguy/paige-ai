## Mission
Build working AI automations for Canadian charities and nonprofits — saving 15+ hours per week, guaranteed. Structured as tax-receipted charitable donations so the charity's operating budget impact is $0.

## Core Offer: The Mission Multiplier
AI automation implementation for Canadian charities in 30 days:
- **Free AI Assessment** — identify at least one automation saving 15+ hours/week
- **First automation live within 2 weeks**
- **Full implementation in 30 days**
- **90 days of support included**
- **Guarantee:** If it doesn't deliver, you don't pay
- **Tax structure:** Services structured as charitable donation eligible for CRA tax receipt

## Target Market
- Canadian registered charities and foundations
- Organizations drowning in admin: grant reporting, donor communications, volunteer scheduling, intake/case management, fundraising follow-up
- Charities where staff spend 60%+ of time on administrative tasks instead of mission work
