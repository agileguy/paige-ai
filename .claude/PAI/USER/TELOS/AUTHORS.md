# Influential Thinkers & Authors

## Stoic Philosophers
- **Marcus Aurelius** — *Meditations*. Duty, systems thinking, leadership under pressure. Ran an empire during a pandemic while writing about service.
- **Seneca** — Letters, essays. Time management, generosity, the shortness of life. Gave his fortune to public works.
- **Epictetus** — *Discourses*, *Enchiridion*. Born a slave, taught that freedom is in how you respond. "First say to yourself what you would be; and then do what you have to do."
- **Cato the Younger** — Led by example. Walked into battle alongside his soldiers. Integrity over expedience.
